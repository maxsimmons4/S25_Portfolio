About

Coursework

Some of my Favorite Courses at Iowa

Applied Wealth Management

Applied Wealth Management is taught by Nathan Koch and fulfills a finance elective. In this class we were broken into groups and assigned a random simulated client. As a group we acted as this client's financial advisor, giving various presentations on background, asset allocation, estate planning, and more. The other part of the class is managing the Marberry Fund through the University of Iowa which is used to help the finance department.

Business Communication and Protocol

In this class I was able to build my communication skills through various assignments and projects. Last summer during my internship I saw this class help as I was more prepared to present in front of company leaders, send emails, and communicate professionally overall.

Digital Product Management

Digital Product Management is taught by Michael Colbert and fulfills a business analytics elective. This class forced me to think in ways I normally do not. Being a double major with finance requires two different ways of thinking. This class gave me more experience with GitHub, as well as introduced me to visual code studio, while being able to grow in my HTML coding.
