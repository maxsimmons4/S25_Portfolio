Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2025

At Max Simmons, accessible from www.maxsimmons.me, your privacy is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by Max Simmons and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect information about your usage of our website. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time you spend on our site. This helps us improve the user experience and understand how visitors use our site.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Analyze and improve the functionality of the website.
  • Enhance user experience based on user behavior.

3. Cookies

Max Simmons uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device, which help us track your interaction with our website. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect your ability to access certain features of our site.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third-party services except for Google Analytics, which may collect and process your data as outlined above. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy for more information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Retention

Data collected by Google Analytics will be retained according to Google's data retention policies. You can find more information about this in Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Security

We strive to use reasonable security measures to protect your personal data, but please be aware that no security system is completely foolproof. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Max Simmons reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
